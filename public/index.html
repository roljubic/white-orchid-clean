Airbnb Algorithm Penalty

A cleanliness rating below 4.8 triggers a search ranking demotion that lasts 60–90 days. On O'ahu, with 180+ days of peak season demand, that demotion costs real bookings — not hypothetical ones.

02

The Complaint You Didn't Know About

Most guests don't complain during their stay — they wait and write a public review. By the time you see "bathroom wasn't properly cleaned," the review is live, your rating has moved, and the next 20 guests have read it.

03

The Maintenance Issue Nobody Flagged

A dripping faucet, a broken screen, a burned-out bulb — cleaners who are just doing a job don't flag these. Our team documents every issue with photos and messages you within an hour. Small problems stay small.

04

Inconsistency Across Turnovers

A cleaning that's great one week and mediocre the next is worse than consistently mediocre — because guests calibrate their expectations to the best experience and feel let down. We use the same team and the same checklist. Every single time.
